✅ Your Final Project Idea (Refined)
💰 Smart Budget & Spending Tracker
A personal finance web app where users:

Set budgets for categories (e.g., Food: ₦30,000/month)

Log actual expenses

Get alerted when spending nears/exceeds the budget

Visualize performance with simple charts

🔍 Key Features
✅ MVP (Doable for CS50)
User registration/login

Create a monthly budget with categories and limits

Add expenses to categories

See how much you’ve spent vs. your budget (percentage)

Visual indicators:

Green (safe), Yellow (near limit), Red (exceeded)

Dashboard summary: e.g., “You’ve spent ₦28,000 of your ₦30,000 Food budget.”
